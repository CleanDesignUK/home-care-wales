Cookie Policy

At Home Care Wales we use small text files called “cookies” to make our site work correctly—but we only set essential cookies required for our contact form to submit. We do not use analytics, advertising or marketing cookies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are tiny data files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember your preferences and ensure essential functionality.

2. Cookies We Use

  • Essential Form Cookies: These cookies allow our contact form to work properly so you can submit your details and we can get back to you.

3. No Analytics or Marketing Cookies

We do not track any usage data for analytics, and we do not set any advertising or third-party marketing cookies on your device.

4. Managing Cookies

You can block or delete cookies at any time via your browser settings, but blocking essential cookies may prevent our contact form from working. See your browser’s “Help” for how to do this.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about our cookie policy, please get in touch.
