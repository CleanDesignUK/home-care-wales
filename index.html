Why Families Choose
Our Home Care Service

At Home Care Wales, families across Anglesey choose us because we combine compassion with reliability and care deeply about the people we support. As a small, community-based service, we offer a highly personalised approach that puts people—not processes—at the centre of everything we do.

We live and work in the same communities as those we support, so we truly understand local needs. Our care is delivered by trained, bilingual staff who provide the Active Offer, meaning Welsh-speaking individuals can receive their care in Welsh without having to ask.

Here’s why families trust us:

  • Consistent, familiar carers – small, steady teams help loved ones build trusted relationships with the same carer.
  • Flexible & responsive – we adapt quickly to changing needs and work closely with families.
  • Transparent communication – families stay informed, involved, and reassured every step of the way.
  • Trusted by local authorities – approved provider on the North Wales framework, delivering care on behalf of Anglesey Council.
  • A personal, compassionate touch – we treat each client as our own family—with dignity, respect, and genuine kindness.

Whether your loved one needs a little help each day or more regular support, Home Care Wales is here to help them live safely and happily at home.

  • Local Anglesey carers you can get to know and trust in person
  • Flexible home care tailored around your daily routine
  • Fast, reliable support for hospital discharge and short-term respite care
  • Clear, upfront pricing with no hidden costs — ever
