Our Story

Home Care Wales was founded in 2018 with a simple but powerful goal — to provide high-quality, person-centred care to the people of Anglesey.

As a small, local service, we’re proud to support our own community with care that’s built on trust, compassion, and dignity. From day one, we’ve focused on delivering reliable, flexible support that truly puts the individual first — whether it’s a few hours of help each week or more regular assistance.

Rooted in Anglesey, shaped by local values, and committed to excellence — we’re here to make a difference, one visit at a time.

Our Mission & Values

We deliver personalised home care and elderly home care with transparency, dignity and respect. Each home caregiver is DBS-checked, trained, and dedicated to treating your loved one like family.
